Famous Tourist Places Around the World

Tokyo

Tokyo, Japan's busy capital, mixes the ultramodern and the traditional, from neon-lit skyscrapers to historic temples.

Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu is an Incan citadel set high in the Andes Mountains in Peru, above the Urubamba River valley.

Colosseum

Rome, Italy - An ancient amphitheatre in Rome, known for its gladiatorial contests and public spectacles.

Christ the Redeemer

Rio de Janeiro, Brazil - A massive statue of Jesus Christ with open arms, located atop Mount Corcovado.

Santorini

Greece - A beautiful island in the Aegean Sea, known for its whitewashed buildings and stunning sunsets.

Petra

Jordan - An archaeological site famous for its rock-cut architecture and water conduit system.
